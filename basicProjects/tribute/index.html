Tom Hanks

Actor-Filmmaker

  • Life Story

  • 1956 : Born at Concord, California, United States
  • 1980 : Made his film debut in the low-budget slasher film "He Knows You're Alone".
  • 1984 : Starred in the Ron Howard directed rom-com "Splash".
  • 1988 : Broad success of the fantasy comedy "Big" established him as a major hollywood talent.
  • 1989 : "First Academy Award nomination for Best Actor in "Big".
  • 1993 : Won the Academy Award for Best Actor in "Philadelphia".
  • 1994 : Won the Academy Award for Best Actor again in "Forrest Gump".
  • 2010-present : Starred in critically acclaimed movies like "Captain Phillips", "Saving Mr.Banks", "Bridge of Spies", "Sully" and also starred in "A Hologram for the King" and "Inferno" among others.
  • He also won the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2016.

"Everybody has something that chews them up and, for me, that thing was always loneliness. The cinema has the power to make you not feel lonely, even when you are." -Tom Hanks
